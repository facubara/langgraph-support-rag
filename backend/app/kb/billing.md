# Billing and Invoices

Invoices are generated on the same day each month based on the plan's billing cycle. Each invoice records an amount, a date, a status, and a short description.

An invoice marked "paid" has been successfully collected. An invoice marked "past_due" means a payment attempt failed and the account may be limited until payment succeeds.

Customers can view their full invoice history, including the invoice id and the charge amount, from their account.
