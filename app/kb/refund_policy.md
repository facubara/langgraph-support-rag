# Refund Policy

Customers on the Pro plan can request a refund within 14 days of a charge. After 14 days, Pro refunds require a human reviewer.

Team plan customers have a 30-day refund window, and refunds are prorated when a customer downgrades mid-cycle.

Duplicate charges are always refundable on the Pro and Team plans, regardless of the refund window.

Enterprise refunds are governed by the customer's contract and always require human review before any money is returned.
