# Duplicate Charges

When a customer is charged twice for the same invoice, verify the duplicate against their invoice history before issuing any refund. Look for two charges with the same amount on the same date.

Confirmed duplicate charges are always refundable on the Pro and Team plans. On the Enterprise plan, a duplicate charge must be reviewed manually by a human agent.

A refund for a duplicate charge still creates a refund ticket, which a human approves before the refund is processed.
